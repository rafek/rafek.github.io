developing

Being in the industry since 2007 and working for several different organisations allowed me to gain a fair amount of experience in a variety of technologies and work environments.

I worked for companies of various sizes and industries. Within teams that were entirely on-site and distributed globally. I started with .NET based desktop applications, then moved to Microsoft web stack where I endured both sides of ASP.NET: WebForms and MVC. After delivering more than a handful of web apps, I jumped into the world of mobile and got involved in native iOS development followed by projects utilising cross-platform solutions like Cordova or Xamarin. I also spent a decent time in the glorious kingdom of JavaScript doing both backend and frontend. As of 2017, I do mostly AR and VR with Windows Mixed Reality platform. Occasional Python backend development is also an excellent springboard for me. In February 2018 I was awarded Microsoft MVP award in Windows Development for my engagement in Microsoft Mixed Reality.

The process of learning will expand your thinking, opening you to new possibilities and new ways of doing things.
